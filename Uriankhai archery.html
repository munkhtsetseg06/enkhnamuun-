URIANHAI ARCHERY

It is said to have originated during the Naadam after the Mongol campaign to the west. There is another rumor that when Subeedei Baatar visited, his mother-in-law during the Tsagaan Sar, he pulled out the rust of his arrow, tied his knot in a knot and played as a team on the river ice. In ancient times, Uriankhai men used to shoot arrows to test their skills.
